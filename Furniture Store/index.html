A wing chair is a classic piece of furniture that exudes elegance and comfort. With its high backrest and distinctive winged sides, it not only provides excellent support but also adds a touch of sophistication to any room. Historically, the design of the wings served a practical purpose by protecting the sitter from cold drafts and helping to retain heat when placed near a fireplace.
